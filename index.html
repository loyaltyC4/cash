Questions

Things creators actually ask.

How long until I see my first takedown?
First scan starts in under 5 minutes. Most creators see their first removed listing within 6-24 hours. Repeat offenders are usually fully de-platformed within 72 hours.
Will pirates know I'm hunting them?
No. Every notice goes out under our DMCA agent name. Pirates see "Striqit Inc." not your real name, address, or contact info. Your identity stays protected even from the people you're hunting.
What if my content's on a site that ignores DMCA?
We escalate. First to the hosting provider. Then to the registrar. Then to payment processors. Then to deindexing. By the time we're done, the site can still exist, but no one can find it, host it, or pay for it.
Does Striqit work for non-adult creators?
Yes. Course creators, photographers, designers, musicians, SaaS founders, and game studios all use Striqit. If you have IP and people are stealing it, we burn it down.
What jurisdictions does it work in?
US DMCA, EU DSA, UK CDPA, Canada CMA, Australia Copyright Act, plus 28 additional national frameworks. We also file under platform-specific policies in jurisdictions without strong IP law.
Can I cancel anytime?
Anytime, no questions, no fees. Your evidence vault stays accessible for 90 days after cancellation in case you decide to come back — or in case you need it in court.
